About Iris Species

  • Iris Setosa: The smallest species. Characterized by narrow leaves and compact structure. Sepal length ranges from 4.3-5.8 cm.
  • Iris Versicolor: Medium-sized species. Has characteristics between setosa and virginica. Sepal length ranges from 5.9-7.0 cm.
  • Iris Virginica: The largest species. Distinguished by wide leaves and long sepal/petal measurements. Sepal length ranges from 6.3-7.9 cm.
